About Me

Terry 'Klaus' Joynson is a cartoonist and illustrator working out of Liverpool with over 20 years' experience.

Klaus usually works with pen and ink on paper, but has also worked with oil paints, watercolours and charcoals. He has contributed to many publications and websites and is open to commissions on any subjects.
